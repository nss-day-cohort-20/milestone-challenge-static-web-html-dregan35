Welcome to David's progression in learning

I started my journey in the tech world when I was fifteen building computers, soldiering modchips into Xbox's or Playstation’s, and sound engineering for churches (which consisted of running 30-100 channel soundboards for various gospel groups). Suddenly everyone says it's my turn to work on me and make my goals come true. Honestly I'm having some trouble with this after devoting so much of my time to others I feel a little selfish, but I know that it is time to make something of my dreams or die trying!
